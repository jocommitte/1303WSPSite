Cascada Location

Cascada luxury apartments in Buckhead

Cascada is located on prestigious West Paces Ferry Road in the heart of Buckhead, Atlanta’s uptown district. Buckhead is often referred to as the “Beverly Hills of the South” because of it’s world class shopping and restaurants. According to Forbes Magazine, it is one of the wealthiest areas in the US and The Robb Report has consistently ranked Buckhead one of the nation’s “10 Top Affluent Communities” due to “the most beautiful mansions, best shopping, and finest restaurants in the Southeastern United States”.
